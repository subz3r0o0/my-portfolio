Spectrum of Sentience - Comic Project

Exploring Artificial Intelligence and Consciousness Through Storytelling

Project Overview

In my third project, I explore the complexities of artificial intelligence (AI) through Eliezer Yudkowsky's Roko's Basilisk, centring on Maya, a protagonist confronting Yudkowsky’s dystopian future dominated by AI singularity. In this world, AI dissenters suffer severe consequences, and Maya, immune to the AI's influence, uncovers her late mother's attempts to avert an AI-driven human extinction. Her journey reaches a climax when she finds a detonation switch to shut down the AI, plunging her into significant moral dilemmas.

This story is intricately woven with the Orthogonality Thesis, which posits that an AI's goals may not align with its intelligence level, highlighting the need for responsible AI development. Maya's interactions with AI and her critical decisions bring to light the potential risks and ethical challenges posed by advanced AI entities, questioning their true motives and impact on humanity.

Themes and Ethical Implications

The narrative critically examines the principles behind Roko's Basilisk, highlighting the need for ethical AI development that prioritises human well-being. It challenges the idea of an omnipotent AI predestined to harm its creators, suggesting this is a pessimistic, potentially harmful perspective.

Inspired by Rodney Brooks and Murray Shanahan, the project promotes a more balanced view of AI's potential and limitations, advocating for proactive ethical engagement, human-centric values, and responsible engineering of AI systems.

Design & Inspirations

Crafting my comic strip, I used RunwayML and Canvas comic templates, emphasising image significance with heavy borders. I integrated contextual details on AI singularity, influenced by Janet Murray's narrative and immersion insights. A significant inspiration for this project is the video game Fallout, known for its post-apocalyptic setting, retro-futuristic design, and ethical dilemmas. This influence shapes the visual storytelling and thematic choices within my comic.

The title of the project, "Spectrum of Sentience," encapsulates its essence—an exploration of the varying levels of consciousness and sentience in humans and AI.
