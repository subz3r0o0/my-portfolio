Data Visualisation & Sensing: Slick Rick's Influence

A data visualisation project highlighting the impact of Slick Rick on the hip-hop industry.

Project Overview

This project showcases the immense influence of Slick Rick, the legendary storyteller of hip hop’s golden age. My interactive data visualisations allow users, especially hip-hop enthusiasts, to explore the vast network of artists who have sampled or drawn inspiration from his iconic tracks.

Visualisations

The first visualisation represents a rotating vinyl record, showing the number of times Slick Rick’s songs have been sampled. The second visualisation displays an interactive circular loop of artist names that have sampled Slick Rick. Finally, the third visualisation breaks down the storytelling elements of his famous track "Children’s Story," allowing users to interact with the song's structure.

Technology Used

The visualisations were created using P5.JS and JavaScript, with data sourced from Genius' API and Wikipedia. These interactive elements offer a visually captivating way to explore Slick Rick’s lasting legacy in the hip-hop genre.
